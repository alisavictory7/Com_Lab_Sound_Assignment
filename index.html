About

The Project

The Colour of Sound invites you into a world where art transcends the visualâ€”where creativity finds new languages when traditional paths are blocked. Follow young Linda, a passionate artist with dreams of painting happiness into the world, as an unexpected twist of fate challenges everything she thought she knew about her artistic journey. When her canvas turns dark, Linda must discover if art lives only in what we see, or if it resonates through all our senses.

This immersive audio experience explores the boundaries between our perceptions, asking: What if colors had sounds? What if art could be felt rather than seen? Through rich soundscapes, emotional performances, and a narrative that branches like life itself, Linda's journey becomes a metaphor for all people facing great, unforeseen difficulties. Whether we choose familiar paths or venture into the unknown, "The Colour of Sound" reminds us that some of the most powerful art can start from what we cannot see, from within.

Listen. Feel. Experience art anew.
